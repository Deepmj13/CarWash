🚀 CarWash FinderCarWash Finder is an online platform designed to connect local car wash shops with customers through a streamlined booking and discovery system. The platform aims to bring offline businesses into the digital space, helping them gain more customers while earning through a commission-based model.  🧠 System OverviewThe platform is designed with three distinct perspectives to ensure a smooth service flow:  User Side: Browse nearby shops, compare ratings and prices, and manage bookings.  Shop Owner Side: Receive and manage booking requests (Accept/Reject) and track earnings.  System Side: Manages automated reminders, calculates shop rankings, and assigns performance badges.  🔄 Core WorkflowShop Onboarding: Convincing local owners to list their shops on the platform.  Discovery: Users browse lists and compare service providers.  Booking: Users select a date and time; the request is marked as "Pending".  Owner Action: Owners accept or reject the booking.  Reminders: Automated messages sent 1–2 hours before the scheduled service.  Service Completion: Status is updated to "Completed" or "No Show".  Feedback: Users provide ratings and reviews only after a completed service.  Ranking: Shops are ranked based on ratings, total bookings, and earned badges.  💰 Business & Commission RulesPayment Model: Initially "Pay at Shop," with manual weekly or monthly commission collection.  Commission Structure:Completed Service: Commission applies.  Cancelled/No Show: No commission charged.  No Show Control: While users aren't charged for missing appointments, the system tracks them; 3 no-shows result in a warning, and 5 lead to a block.  🏆 Badge SystemThe platform rewards high-performing shops with specific badges to improve their ranking:  Top Performer: Awarded for high booking volumes.  Best Rated: Awarded for maintaining high user ratings.  Fast Service: Awarded for quick booking response times.  📅 RoadmapPhase 1: Onboard 5–10 shops and launch the basic booking system.  Phase 2: Implement the review and rating system.  Phase 3: Roll out the badge and ranking logic.  Phase 4: Integrate digital payments and automated commission tracking.
